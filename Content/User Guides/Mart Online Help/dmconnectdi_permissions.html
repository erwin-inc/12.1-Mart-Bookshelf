erwin DM Connect for DI Permissions

To use DM Connect for DI to push data into Data Catalog and Business Glossary, you need certain permissions on erwin Mart Administrator and erwin Data Intelligence Suite (DI Suite).

Minimum erwin Mart Permissions

You can use DM Connect for DI only if your account has been assigned Administrator or Architect profiles.
