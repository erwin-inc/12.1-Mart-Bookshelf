Home Page

The Home page is a configurable wiki-like landing page, where you can display key information for business users, important images, hyperlinks, text, and more.

To configure the Home page, follow these steps:

  1. Click .

  2. On the Write tab, use HTML syntax to edit the page.
  3. Click Preview to preview your changes.
  4. Click Save.

To revert the changes that you made, click Reset.
